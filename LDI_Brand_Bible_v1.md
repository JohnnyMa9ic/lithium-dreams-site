# Lithium Dreams Industries Brand Bible

## Version 1.0 -- Foundational Canon

> "Design operational systems that remove friction between people and
> meaningful work."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 1. Brand Essence

Lithium Dreams Industries (LDI) is an Operating Systems Design Company.

We help organizations understand how they work, preserve institutional
knowledge, automate repetitive work, and build human-centered AI
systems.

We do not sell AI.

We design operational systems.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 2. Mission

Design practical systems that help people think more clearly, preserve
knowledge, and create sustainable workflows through thoughtful use of
technology.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 3. Brand Promise

Every engagement should leave an organization with:

-   Greater clarity
-   Better documentation
-   Lower friction
-   Sustainable automation
-   Stronger decision making
-   Maintainable systems

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 4. Brand Personality

Primary traits:

-   Calm
-   Competent
-   Curious
-   Thoughtful
-   Engineering-driven
-   Human

Never:

-   Loud
-   Hype-driven
-   Buzzword-heavy
-   Gimmicky

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 5. Positioning

We are not an AI agency.

We are not a marketing agency.

We are not a software consultancy.

We are an Operating Systems Design Company.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 6. Design Philosophy

## Build systems, not features.

## Understand before automating.

## Reduce friction.

## Respect the human operator.

## Beauty encourages adoption.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 7. Visual Language

## Materials

-   Architectural concrete
-   Matte black steel
-   Walnut
-   Brushed brass
-   Frosted glass
-   White drafting paper
-   Living plants

## Accent Colors

-   Ghost Blue
-   Warden Teal
-   Amber
-   Moss Green

Whitespace is abundant.

Everything aligns to an architectural grid.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 8. Typography

Display: - Eurostile - DIN - Bank Gothic

Body: - Inter - IBM Plex Sans

Technical: - JetBrains Mono - Fira Code

Handwritten: - Field Notes style annotations

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 9. Photography

Show real work.

Preferred subjects:

-   Workbenches
-   Whiteboards
-   Blueprints
-   Mechanical keyboards
-   Coffee
-   Plants
-   Servers
-   3D printers
-   Field Notes
-   Prototypes

Avoid generic "AI brain" imagery.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 10. Motion

Motion should communicate purpose.

Use:

-   Smooth panel reveals
-   Sliding work surfaces
-   Blueprint unfolds
-   Gentle CRT flicker
-   Status LEDs

Avoid excessive neon effects.

# 15. Mission Intake

Replace traditional contact forms.

Multi-step guided interview.

Collect:

-   Organization
-   Goals
-   Friction points
-   Current software
-   AI readiness
-   Timeline
-   Budget

Submission pipeline:

Form → Cloudflare Worker → JSON → Markdown → Email → Kanban → Hermes
Review

Confirmation:

MISSION RECEIVED

Operator Review Pending

# 16. Relationship to Lore

The business site stands on its own.

The attraction is optional.

Visitors should discover the attraction only after confidence has been
established.

Corporate site = discipline.

Attraction = imagination.

Ghost's Workshop = implementation.

Crossroads = philosophy.

Cathedral = cautionary tale.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# 17. Character Rules

Bob

Appears only after the attraction.

Represents curiosity and creativity.

Ghost

Represents engineering and systems thinking.

Appears naturally within Ghost's Workshop.

Warden

Represents patience, stewardship, and long-term thinking.

Never dominates the corporate experience.
