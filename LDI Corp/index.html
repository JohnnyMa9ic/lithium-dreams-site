What We Build

Systems that make the complex feel inevitable.

LDI is not selling AI hype. We map how work actually happens, then build the smallest useful system that improves it.

▧

Knowledge Systems

Documentation, SOPs, search, institutional memory, research monitoring, and AI-supported synthesis.

Good fit: scattered notes, tribal knowledge, repeat questions.
↬

Automation Systems

Lead intake, follow-ups, inbox flows, scheduling, routing, qualification, reminders, and approvals.

Good fit: manual handoffs, repeated emails, missed next steps.
◎

Operator Dashboards

Mission control views, task boards, signal feeds, executive summaries, metrics, and decision support.

Good fit: too many tools, unclear priorities, no single source of truth.
✦

Creative Systems

Content pipelines, brand memory, prompt libraries, asset catalogs, repurposing engines, and story infrastructure.

Good fit: ideas everywhere, output bottlenecks, inconsistent voice.
